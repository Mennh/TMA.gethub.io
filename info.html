Turath International School Curriculum Overview:


Educational Philosophy:
ABC International School follows a holistic approach to education, emphasizing academic excellence, character
development, and global citizenship. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and a
love for lifelong learning.

Accreditation:
The school is accredited by [Cis], ensuring adherence to international educational standards.

Grade Levels:
Turath International School offers programs from Kindergarten through Grade 12.

Curriculum Framework:
Early Years (Kindergarten to Grade 2):
Focus on foundational skills: language development, numeracy, and social-emotional learning.
Play-based learning to stimulate curiosity and creativity.

Primary Years (Grade 3 to Grade 5):
Core subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies.
Introduction to specialized subjects such as Art, Music, and Physical Education.
Emphasis on inquiry-based learning and project-based assessments.

Middle Years (Grade 6 to Grade 8):
Integration of core subjects with a more in-depth exploration of disciplines.
Introduction to a second language.
Emphasis on critical thinking and research skills.

High School (Grade 9 to Grade 12):
College preparatory curriculum with a wide range of subject choices.
Advanced Placement (AP) courses available.
Career guidance and counseling for university admissions.

Languages:
English is the primary language of instruction. Arabic is taught as a second language, and additional language
options are available.



Specialized Programs:
STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics):
Integration of STEM principles across the curriculum.
Hands-on projects and experimentation.

Global Studies:
Emphasis on global issues, multicultural understanding, and international perspectives.
Extracurricular Activities:
A diverse range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and clubs, to enrich students'
experiences.

Assessment and Reporting:
A variety of assessment methods, including continuous evaluation, standardized testing, and project-based
assessments. Regular parent-teacher conferences and detailed progress reports.

Graduation Requirements:
Completion of required credits and successful fulfillment of academic and extracurricular criteria.

Beyond Academics:
ABC International School places a strong emphasis on character development, community service, and preparing
students for responsible global citizenship.
