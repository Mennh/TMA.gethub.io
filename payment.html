Outstanding Scholarship Program.

Eligibility Criteria: Open to students with exceptional academic achievements, leadership skills, and extracurricular involvement
.Typically available for students entering a specific grade level (e.g., high school).

Application Process: Interested students must submit a scholarship application along with academic transcripts
, letters of recommendation, and a personal statement.

Selection Process: A scholarship committee reviews applications and selects recipients
based on academic performance,leadership potential, and extracurricular accomplishments.

Scholarship Benefits: Full or partial tuition coverage for the academic year.
Additional benefits such as waived registration fees, free textbooks, or access to special programs.

Duration: Scholarships are often awarded on an annual basis and may be renewable based on the student's continuedacademic
excellence and involvement in school activities.

Ongoing Criteria: Scholars are expected to maintain a high level of academic achievement and actively participate in school activities.




Special cases fund program

Purpose: The Special Cases Fund is established to provide financial assistance to families facing unexpected challenges,
such as job loss, medical emergencies, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Eligibility Criteria: Families experiencing financial hardship due to unexpected events.
Submission of a detailed application outlining the nature of the special circumstance and its financial impact.

Application Process: Families submit a confidential application to the school's financial aid or special cases committee.
The application includes documentation supporting the special circumstance, such as medical bills, termination
notices, or other relevant documents.

Review Process: A committee reviews applications and assesses the validity and severity of the special circumstance.
Decisions are made based on a combination of need and available funds in the Special Cases Fund.

Assistance Provided: Financial assistance may cover tuition fees, school supplies, or other necessary expenses.
The assistance provided is determined on a case-by-case basis.

Confidentiality: All information related to applications and assistance provided is kept confidential to respect the privacy of
families facing challenging circumstances.
